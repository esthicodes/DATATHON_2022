FAQ

Will Data Days be an online or on-site event?
The Data Days will take place on-site at ETH Zurich. We will provide you with space to meet up and work as a team and food & beverages so that you can focus entirely on your challenges.Who is eligible to participate in the Datathon?
The hackathon is open to all ETH and UZH students. Places are distributed on a first-come-first-serve basis. The overall participation number is capped at 100 participants to ensure a positive overall experience for all involved parties. Do I need to apply to the AI fair?
No, you are free to visit the AI fair spontaneously. In contrast, an application for the Datathon is needed.How many participants are allowed per team?
You will register together with at least one other person and work in team sizes of 3 or 4. Team members are registered in the application form. Only one application per team is needed.Are there fees to participate in the fair or hackathon?
There are no fees to participate.

Do I need technical skills to participate in the Datathon?
Due to the technical nature of the challenge, basic coding skills and familiarity with machine learning would be recommended to ensure a positive experience. However, there are no formal requirements.

SAV

The Swiss Association of Actuaries SAA is the professional body of Swiss actuaries and was
founded in 1905.
The purpose of the association is to promote and developpe actuarial mathematics and related areas such as financial mathematics and enterprise risk management and their competent application, as well as to represent the professional interests of actuaries. Main responsibilities include issuing recommendations for actuarial training, regular publication of scientific work, providing actuarial and actuarial principles and recommendations, as well as influencing discussions and decisions in politics and the Economics, in which actuarial and underwriting considerations are to be included.
The SAA maintains contacts with professional associations in other countries and is a full
member of the international umbrella organization, the International Actuarial Association
(IAA), and at European level of the Actuarial Association of Europe (AAE).
